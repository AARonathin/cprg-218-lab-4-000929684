FAQ's

Booking a trip with Stellar Transit Services is as simple as visiting our website and selecting the "Book a Trip" option. From there, you can choose your destination, departure date, and cabin class. After filling in some personal information and completing the payment process, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details of your upcoming space adventure. If you need assistance or have special requirements, our customer service team is available 24/7 to help you.

Your safety is our utmost priority. All our spacecraft are engineered to the highest safety standards and undergo rigorous testing and regular maintenance. Our crew members are highly trained professionals, equipped to handle any situation. We adhere to strict safety protocols, including comprehensive pre-flight checks, in-flight monitoring, and emergency preparedness drills. Additionally, all passengers will participate in a safety briefing before departure, covering essential information and procedures.

We understand that plans can change, and we strive to be as flexible as possible. Passengers can cancel or modify their bookings up to 30 days before the scheduled departure without any penalty. For changes or cancellations made within 30 days of departure, a fee may apply. To cancel or change your booking, please contact our customer service team, who will be happy to assist you. We also offer travel insurance options for additional peace of mind.
